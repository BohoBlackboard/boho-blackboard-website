About Me

Hi! I'm Danielle — a 27-year-old teacher who loves creating warm, beautiful learning spaces. Designing my own classroom décor became one of the greatest joys of my teaching journey, and now I get to share it with others who love the same boho-inspired calm and creativity.

Boho Blackboard was born out of a simple wish: *to help teachers feel proud, inspired and excited every time they walk into their classroom.* Whether you're decorating a brand-new room or refreshing your space, I hope my themes and printables bring you joy and make your classroom feel like home.
